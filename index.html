Global production of plastics has increased by more than twenty-fold between 1964 and 2015

The world produces 381 million tonnes in plastic waste yearly – this is set to double by 2034 and almost quadruple by 2050.

50% of this is single- use plastic & only 9% has ever been recycled.

Plastics stay in the environment for a long time, with some taking up to 500 years to break down.

In Africa and the entire world plastic waste is a ticking time bomb.

SPCTA is an end-to-end plastic waste value chain development enterprise. We apply circular economy principles to collect, process, recycle and reuse plastic waste.

We are building an inclusive community-based, micro-collection and processing network across Africa to create One Million youth jobs.
